HOW TO ADD YOUR OWN PHOTOS:

1.  Paste your images into this folder (`public/memories`).
2.  Rename them to match the planet names (case insensitive):
    - mercury.jpg (or mercury.png)
    - venus.jpg   (or venus.png)
    - earth.jpg   ...
    - mars.jpg
    - jupiter.jpg
    - saturn.jpg
    - uranus.jpg
    - neptune.jpg

Supported formats: .jpg, .png.

If a photo is missing or named incorrectly, the app will show a random "memory" instead.
